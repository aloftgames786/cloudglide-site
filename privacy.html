Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

CloudGlide is developed by Aloft Games. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data We Collect

CloudGlide does not collect, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information. All game data (scores, settings, purchased items, coins, gems) is stored locally on your device only using Android's SharedPreferences and is never sent to any server.

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Camera, microphone, or contacts

In-App Purchases

CloudGlide offers optional in-app purchases (gem packs). All payments are processed entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store any payment information such as credit card numbers or billing addresses.

Third-Party Services

The current version of CloudGlide does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or social login SDKs. Future updates may include ad services (e.g. AppLovin MAX) and this policy will be updated accordingly before any such update is released.

Children's Privacy

CloudGlide is rated for ages 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. Since the app collects no personal data at all, it is safe for all age groups.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
